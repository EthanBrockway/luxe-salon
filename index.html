Luxe Salon Ladies

Meghan

Meghan is a hairstylist and lash tech that graduated cosmetology school from Z- Hair Academy. She knew she wanted to be a hairstylist because she’s loved doing hair since she was a little girl. She believes that everyone deserves to look as good as they feel and wants to help people love what they see when they look in the mirror. Meghan’s favorite services to perform are dimensional color and eyelash extensions. Expanding her knowledge and learning new techniques is something she’s very passionate about. Meghan has furthered her education by taking additional classes and becoming a certified balayage specialist through Sunlight's Pro and an eyelash technician through Prolash Products!

When she is not doing hair, Meghan spends her free time by having family game nights, hanging out with friends, going to the gym, volunteering, and staying active in church. Shes a Thrillseeker that’s always looking for her next adventure! Meghan is excited to see you in her chair for all of your hair needs!

Cara

Cara is a graduate from Z-hair Academy on Barry Road. She has loved all things beauty since she was a little girl. Cara has a passion for connecting with others while also making them feel beautiful inside and out! Continuing her education is something that is very important to Cara, and she is always excited to keep up with the new trends. After she completed cosmetology school, she furthered her education and became a certified lash tech through Prolash! Cara enjoys all things blonding and dimensional color and is excited to continue expanding her skills into other parts of the industry.

When Cara isn’t in the salon, she enjoys volunteering at her local church and other events. She also likes to spend time in nature, workout at the gym, visit with her family and friends, and travel whenever she has the chance! Cara is looking forward to having you in her chair for all of your hair and beauty needs!
